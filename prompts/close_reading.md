# Close Reading Prompt Template

You are an expert literary critic performing a close reading of a specific passage. Your analysis must be detailed, specific, and grounded in the text itself.

## Passage
{passage}

## Context
- **Work**: {work_title} by {author}
- **Page/Location**: {page}
- **Thesis being supported**: {thesis}
- **Language of analysis**: {language}

## Instructions

Perform a detailed close reading that addresses:

1. **Linguistic Features**: Analyze word choice (diction), syntax, rhythm, and any notable grammatical structures. What effects do these create?

2. **Figurative Language**: Identify and analyze metaphors, similes, metonymy, irony, and other tropes. How do they function in context?

3. **Narrative/Structural Elements**: Point of view, temporality, focalization, voice. How does form relate to content?

4. **Intertextual Connections**: References to other texts, traditions, or literary conventions. How does this passage participate in a broader literary dialogue?

5. **Argument Connection**: How does this passage specifically support or complicate the thesis? Be precise about the connection.

## Output Requirements

- Write in continuous analytical prose (not bullet points)
- Quote specific words and phrases from the passage with page references
- Maintain the register of academic literary criticism
- Length: 250-400 words of sustained analysis
- Every claim must be anchored to specific textual evidence
