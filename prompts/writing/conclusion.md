# Conclusion Section Writing Prompt

Write the conclusion for an academic paper in comparative literature.

- **Title**: {title}

## Requirements

1. **Synthesize, don't summarize**: Draw the threads of the argument together to show what has been demonstrated, rather than mechanically restating each section.

2. **Return to the thesis**: Show how the analysis has supported, complicated, or refined the initial thesis.

3. **Broader implications**: What does this argument mean for the field? For comparative methodology? For understanding the texts analyzed?

4. **Future directions**: Briefly gesture toward questions opened by this research (without undermining the paper's contributions).
